Who Are We

Welcome to Bake Smart, an award-winning bakery nestled in the heart of Johannesburg. Founded on October 28, 2024, by dynamic couple Khensani and Bethuel, Bake Smart was born from a shared love for baking and a vision to create luxurious, unforgettable treats. We specialize crafting luxurious and delicious treats, as well as crafting exquisite cakes and desserts for weddings, birthdays, baby showers, and all of life’s special moments. More than just a bakery, we’re also a hub for aspiring bakers—offering professional baking classes designed to inspire and empower

Every treat we create is thoughtfully made to delight the senses, blending indulgent ingredients with artistic flair.

Delivery Information

We offer reliable delivery across Johannesburg to bring our luxurious treats straight to your door. Whether you're ordering for a celebration, a corporate event, or a personal indulgence, we ensure your baked goods arrive fresh, beautifully packaged, and on time.

• Delivery Areas: Johannesburg and surrounding suburbs

• Delivery Days: Monday to Saturday

• Delivery Times: Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to traffic, weather, or demand.

• Order Cut-Off: Place orders by 5PM for next-day delivery

• Fees: R50 flat rate within 15km radius; custom quote for longer distances

• Same-Day Delivery: Available for select items—subject to availability

• Packaging: All items are carefully boxed to preserve freshness and presentation

• Tracking: Receive real-time updates once your order is dispatched. Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery information.

Need something urgent or custom? Reach out to our team—we’re happy to accommodate special requests whenever possible.
