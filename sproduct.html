Angel Chocolate Cake

Product Details

Tiramisu-inspired chocolate cream cake, featuring a rich chocolate base topped with alternating layers of light sponge cake and creamy mascarpone or whipped cream. The top is dusted generously with cocoa powder, adding a subtle bitterness that balances the sweetness of the cream. Its smooth, creamy texture combined with the moist chocolate foundation makes it ideal for dessert lovers, dinner parties, or anyone with an eye for food photography.
